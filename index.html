The Splash of Lives — an Indian campus coming-of-age web novel

The Splash of Lives is a warm online web novel reader experience set in India. Follow three freshers as a wrong GPS turn leads him to Splash, a small WiFi-free café filled with filter coffee, quiet conversations, older regulars, and the slow feeling of belonging. Read the story episodes online and meet the characters through this immersive reader by Ayushman Gouda under SOUL Productions.
