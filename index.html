The Splash of Lives — An Indian Campus Web Novel

Three freshers. One college. Absolutely no idea what they're doing.

Kanishk Ranjan from Madhya Pradesh — silver-tongued, chronically broke, and dangerously persuasive. Joseph Pinto from Madras — genuinely wants to study but keeps ending up in situations. Somyonil Roy from Bengal — athletic, fiercely loyal, and completely useless around girls.

They arrive at St. Cross College of Technology in Maharashtra with separate lives, separate problems, and no idea they're on a collision course. This is the prologue. This is where it all begins.
